About me

Hello, my name is Sathvik Pandu, and I'm interested in the intersection of
Engineering with Fashion Product Development to deliver quality products.

Fashion product development involves research and inspiration, concept sketching, creating technical specifications, evaluating materials, and implementing sustainability considerations. The integration of technical engineering principles with fashion design is essential for creating high-quality, functional products that meet market demands. My background in mechanical engineering and web design allows me to contribute efficiently across multiple aspects of this process, from design conceptualization to final production.

I focus on innovative solutions by streamlining the product development process, ensuring materials are both aesthetically pleasing and durable. My aim is to bring technological efficiency and creative design together, using modern tools such as Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) software to enhance product quality. This combination of engineering and design principles will push the boundaries of fashion product development, resulting in market-ready, sustainable, and innovative designs.
